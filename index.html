Longtao Zheng
longtao001 at e.ntu.edu.sg

I am a Ph.D student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Bo An.

I received Bachelor of Computer Science at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in June 2022. Before NTU, I was advised by wonderful mentors: Dr. Yujing Hu at NetEase Fuxi AI Lab, Prof. Jianmin Ji at USTC Robotics Lab, and Prof. Fei Chiang at McMaster University.

My research interests span RL and language agents.
